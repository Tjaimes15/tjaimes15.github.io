A little bit about me!

I was born and raised in NYC. I moved to Bucaramanga, Colombia when I was 12 to complete my middle and high school years. I graduated from high school in 2018 with an emphasis in science and mathematics, with a determination to use engineering as a means to be the change I wanted to see in my community. As such, I moved back to NYC to pursue my undergraduate career; and majored in Mechatronic Engineering due to its multidisciplinary engineering application by combining mechanical and electrical engineering, along with coding. This interdisciplinary field allowed me to be part of the full life-cycle of projects, which I immediately fell in love with! And since 2019, I've been involved in numerous teams to invent solutions that better my community's living or to build competitive robots and rovers!
